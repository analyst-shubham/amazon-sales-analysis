# Amazon Sales Analysis (SQL + Excel)

## Objective
Analyze Amazon sales data to understand business performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends using SQL and Excel.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Preview

![Dashboard](amazon_dashboard.jpg)

---

## Key Insights

- Total revenue reached ₹9.18 Cr across 100K orders  
- Sales are evenly distributed across categories (~16–17%), indicating no heavy dependency on a single category  
- No single product dominates — top products contribute similar share (~20% each)  
- CoreTech and KiddoFun are leading brands, but overall brand competition is balanced  
- Credit and Debit Cards are the dominant payment methods, with UPI also widely used  
- Monthly revenue trend is stable with minor fluctuations, showing consistent demand  
- Electronics remains a consistently strong category across multiple years  

---

## Tools Used

- PostgreSQL (Data Analysis)
- Microsoft Excel (Dashboard & Visualization)

## Project Overview

This project focuses on exploring and analyzing sales data to answer key business questions and generate useful insights.

---

## Key Business Questions

- What is total revenue, orders, and units sold?  
- Which category generates the most revenue?  
- Which products generate highest revenue?  
- Which brands dominate sales?  
- How does revenue change over time?  
- Which payment method is most used?  
- Which category performs best each year?
